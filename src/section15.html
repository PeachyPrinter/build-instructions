Section 15 - Calibrating the Peachy Printer

For this section you will need:
  • The Peachy Printer with all connected to your computer with software installed, your upper and lower reservoirs, and the drip feed
  • Calibration grid
  • Extra elastic thread (about 9 inches in length)
  • One small nut (from the nuts and bolts bag)
  • Wire mesh print base
  • Saltwater
  • The extra syringe
  • Safety glasses


Click to see an image of the parts labelled.

Safety Note: This section of the instructions requires the laser to be on and unguarded. Everyone within line of sight of the printer should be wearing the UV protective glasses provided. If you have pets, make sure they are out of the room. Remember, you can't see the UV light very well. Even a very bright UV light will appear fairly dim to the human eye unless its shining on a phosphorescent surface. It is possible for other sounds from your computer to turn the laser on (music, notifications, etc) so ensure that all other sounds are muted when using your Peachy Printer.

1. For calibration instructions refer to the help documents in the software by clicking the “?” button in the top right corner of the calibration menus in the software.
