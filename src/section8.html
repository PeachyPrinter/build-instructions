Section 8 - Making the laser focus adjustable

Description: In this section you will be taking apart the laser, adding an aperture disc, and ensuring that the lasers focus ring can indeed adjust the focus.

Adding an aperture limits the amount of light and the size of the beam leaving the laser allowing us to print thin and properly cured walls.

For this section you will need:
  • Laser
  • Laser Aperture Disc
  • Glue stick
  • CA Glue

    • Click to see an image of the parts labelled.

1. Place the laser aperture disc of the lens on the front of the laser. Position it so that the hole in the disc is centered on the laser.
2. Place a drop of glue on the edge of the Aperture Disc, being careful not to move the disc at all. Use your glue stick to spread the glue all the way around the outer edge of the disc. Make sure that the hole in the disc is centered on the laser and let the glue set.
