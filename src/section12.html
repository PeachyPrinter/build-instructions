Section 12 - Mounting the Laser Assembly to the Main Hanger

Description: In this step you'll be mounting the laser assembly to the main hanger. The main hanger is what connects the internal workings of the printer to the internal housing.
For this section you will need:
  • The Main hanger
  • The Laser assembly (The L bracket with the Circuit, Laser, and Mirror assemblies mounted.)
  • Stand Off
  • Tweezers

Click to see an image of the parts labelled.

1. Connect the Laser Assembly to the Main Hanger by inserting the Stand off arm and Circuit Mounting Plate into mounting slots 7 and 8 respectively. The 7 on the Circuit Mounting Plate should be facing the 7 on the Main Hanger, and the 8 on the Stand off arm should be facing the 8 on the Main Hanger.
2. Use tweezers to remove the protective film from the reflective side of the mirror. Be very careful not to touch the mirror with your fingers.
