Section 4 - Mounting the Mirror Assemblies to the L Bracket

Description: In this section you'll be mounting both mirror assemblies to the L bracket. The L bracket is the piece that holds the laser, the circuit, and the mirror assemblies together in one piece.

For this section you will need:
  • Both Mirror Assemblies
  • The L Bracket
  • Static damper mount B (Mirror frame mount B)
